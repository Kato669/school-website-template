our libray

Our Library is a great facility comprising of the student’s and staff section. It enhances the student’s academic and life spheres through adventurous and personal discovery from our colossal collection. Shelving about 15000 books, It is also spacious and comfortable due to the condusive environment giving the reader a comfortable and convenient service.

The library collection is organized according to international standards, that is to say, Dewy Decimal Classification system and catalogued with Anglo American Cataloguing rules 2nd edition thus locating something to read is a few seconds away. Fresh and selected newspapers from the printing press is a daily habit for our Library preventing current affairs decay for the students not forgetting a bunch of selected magazines and general information resources. These include Dictionaries, World Books, Atlases, Guinness World Book of Records, Independent magazines, Almanacs, Encyclopedias, Thesaurus, National Geographic, Bulletins from (Bank of Uganda, Parliament, National Environmental Management Authority, National Water and Sewerage Corporation, Uganda Revenue Authority, The Constitution of the Republic of Uganda etc.)

our ICT lib

St. Sydotech SS envisages the rapid transformation of the 21st century. With that in mind, ICT is at the forefront of our academic and co-curricular activities. With a fully equipped IT Lab housing the state of the art computers and modern technology with internet connectivity, we are confident about the future of our students being able to adapt with the dynamic workplace with this prior exposure.

The school is highly concerned with the online safety of students. We promote responsible technology use as highlighted in our rules and regulations.Our resolute school firewall and devices with filters ensure that students are protected from the colossal dangers online. This gives them comfort while browsing for online resources that complement their learning.
